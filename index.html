Learn About Me

I'm a Bachelor in computer science by Federal Technological University of Paraná. I worked on projects in several areas during graduation. Among these areas there are subjects related to collaborative systems, smart cities, microcontrolled systems, accessible computing for blind people and software engineering.

My focus is on software engineering, specifically topics related to development and agile methodologies, maintenance and software quality. My research, development and interest focused on the metric called conceptual coupling, a measure used in software engineering activities in day-to-day.
